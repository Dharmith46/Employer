Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account? ▶

Creating an account is simple. Click on the 'Register' button on the top right corner and fill in the required details.

How do I apply for a job? ▶

Once you have created an account, you can browse job listings and click 'Apply' on any job that interests you.

How do I reset my password? ▶

To reset your password, click on 'Forgot Password' on the login page and follow the instructions.
